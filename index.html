Monasteries of Sikkim

Gonjang Monastery

Sikkim, India

Gonjang Monastery, founded in 1981 near Gangtok, is a Nyingma Buddhist monastery known for its peace, culture, and scenic views.

Learn More

Sinon Monastery

Sikkim, India

Sinon Monastery, near Gangtok in Sikkim, is a historic Buddhist monastery known for its traditional architecture and spiritual significance.

Learn More

Phensang Monastery

Sikkim, India

Phensang Monastery in Sikkim, built in 1721, is a Nyingma Buddhist monastery renowned for its annual festival with sacred dances.

Learn More

Rumtek Monastery

Sikkim, India

Rumtek Monastery near Gangtok is Sikkim's largest monastery, serving as the main seat of the Karmapa and a center of Tibetan Buddhism.

Learn More

Pemayangetse Monastery

Sikkim, India

Pemayangtse Monastery, one of Sikkim's oldest, is a major Nyingma monastery famed for its ancient relics and stunning Himalayan views.

Learn More

Phodong Monastery

Sikkim, India

Phodong Monastery in North Sikkim, built in the 18th century, is a Kagyu Buddhist monastery noted for its vibrant festivals and sacred dances.
